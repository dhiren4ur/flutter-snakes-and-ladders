# Privacy Policy for Snakes & Ladders: Twisted Edition

**Last Updated: October 09, 2025**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Snakes & Ladders: Twisted Edition ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Game statistics, session duration, feature usage
- **Technical Data**: App performance metrics, crash reports

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- Personal names or contact information
- Location data
- Photos, videos, or files from your device
- Contacts or social media accounts
- Payment information (our app is free)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
- Display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob
- Ensure app security and prevent abuse

### Google Play Services

- **Privacy Policy**: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:
- Data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
- We do not store personal information on our servers
- Third-party services follow their own security standards

## Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for children under 13 and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act):
- We do not knowingly collect personal information from children
- No registration or account creation is required
- Advertisements are family-friendly and age-appropriate

## Data Retention

- We do not retain personal user data
- Anonymous usage statistics may be kept for app improvement purposes
- Third-party services retain data according to their own policies

## Your Rights

# Privacy Policy Hosting Options

## Quick & Free Options for Hosting Your Privacy Policy

### Option 1: GitHub Pages (Recommended)
1. Create a GitHub repository: `snakes-ladders-privacy`
2. Upload your privacy policy as `index.html`
3. Enable GitHub Pages in repository settings
4. Your URL will be: `https://yourusername.github.io/snakes-ladders-privacy/`

### Option 2: Google Sites
1. Go to sites.google.com
2. Create a new site
3. Add your privacy policy content
4. Publish and get your URL

### Option 3: Blogger/Blogspot
1. Create a free blog at blogger.com
2. Create a single post with your privacy policy
3. URL format: `https://yourappname.blogspot.com/privacy-policy`
